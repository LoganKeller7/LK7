NFL Teams and Records

NFL Teams and Records is a website where it shows the records of all NFL teams. Founded in 2019 by 14 year old Logan Keller

Mantra NFL Teams and Records is a website where it shows the records of all NFL team in the 2018-2019 football season and where they rank. It also shows the records of teams throught out the 2019-2020 football season. It also shows where teams rank in their division so you can see who is on top.
